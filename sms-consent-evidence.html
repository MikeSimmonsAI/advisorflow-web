SMS Consent Documentation — Restland Cemetery & Funeral Home

This page documents the opt-in consent process for A2P 10DLC campaign verification purposes.

This page is maintained by Mike Simmons, Family Service Advisor at Restland Cemetery & Funeral Home, Dallas, TX, for the purpose of providing publicly verifiable evidence of SMS opt-in consent collection methods used in the BookaBoost / AdvisorFlow appointment scheduling platform.

Business Overview

Restland Cemetery & Funeral Home is a licensed funeral and cemetery service provider located in Dallas, TX. Mike Simmons (Family Service Advisor) sends appointment scheduling and follow-up SMS messages to existing customers and prospects who have explicitly consented to receive such communications. Messages include scheduling links, appointment reminders, and follow-up outreach related to pre-need and at-need funeral and cemetery planning services.

Opt-In Consent Paths

Path 1

Website / Online Booking Form

Customers who visit https://advisorflow-booking.vercel.app and schedule an appointment are presented with the following consent checkbox on the booking form. The checkbox is unchecked by default and must be actively selected by the user before the form can be submitted.

I agree to receive SMS text messages from Restland Cemetery & Funeral Home regarding appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-up communications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. View our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
Consent checkbox is unchecked by default (active opt-in required). Form URL: https://advisorflow-booking.vercel.app/book/[advisor-slug] Privacy Policy: https://advisorflow-booking.vercel.app/privacy-policy Terms & Conditions: https://advisorflow-booking.vercel.app/terms
Path 2

Offline / In-Person & Phone Verbal Consent

Customers who provide their mobile phone number during an in-person consultation, phone inquiry, or scheduled file review appointment at Restland are verbally informed of SMS communications at the time of number collection. The advisor reads the following standardized disclosure:

Verbal Disclosure Script (read by advisor at time of number collection): "By providing your mobile phone number, you agree to receive SMS text messages from Restland Cemetery & Funeral Home regarding appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-up communications related to your funeral or cemetery planning services. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance. Your number will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Do you consent to receive these messages?" Consent is only recorded upon affirmative verbal confirmation ("yes" or equivalent).

This offline consent process is documented internally. Consent records include: customer name, date of consent, advisor name, and opt-in method. These records are maintained in the AdvisorFlow CRM system.

Required Disclosures (Both Paths)

  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Message frequency varies
  • Reply STOP to opt out
  • Reply HELP for assistance
  • No mobile numbers are shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes
